The $14K insight applied to wtkpro.site: Ghulam Ali's site sold for $14K because it had topical authority (Google trusted it as the go-to resource for its niche), consistent content clusters, and internal linking that made every page reinforce every other page. Right now wtkpro.site has tools but almost no content. A site with 40+ tools and 0 supporting blog posts looks like a tool dump to Google — not an authority. Publishing 2 blog posts per week for 12 weeks, tightly clustered around your existing tools, is the single highest-ROI activity you can do right now. Each blog post that links to a tool is a free backlink to that tool from your own domain.
